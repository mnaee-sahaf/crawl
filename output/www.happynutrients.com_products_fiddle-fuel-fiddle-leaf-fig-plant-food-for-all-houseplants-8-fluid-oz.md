Growing Tips 
### **Provide Adequate Light**
Place your fiddle leaf fig in bright, indirect light near a window.
### **Water Properly**
Water when the top inch of soil is dry and ensure good drainage.
### **Maintain Humidity**
Use a humidifier, mist the leaves, or place a water tray nearby to increase humidity.
### **Use Fiddle Fuel Fertilizer**
Feed with Fiddle Fuel fertilizer, dissolving the recommended amount in water and applying as needed.
### **Prune and Clean the Leaves**
Prune to maintain shape and remove dead leaves, and clean leaves with a damp cloth regularly.
